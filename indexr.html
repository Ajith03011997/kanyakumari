Welcome to Kanyakumari

Kanyakumari is known for its stunning sunsets, beaches, temples, and cultural heritage. A beautiful place where the Arabian Sea, Bay of Bengal, and Indian Ocean meet.

About Kanyakumari

Kanyakumari, formerly known as Cape Comorin, is a district located at the southern tip of the Indian subcontinent. It is renowned for its rich cultural heritage and stunning landscapes.

Geography

The district is surrounded by the Arabian Sea, Bay of Bengal, and the Indian Ocean. It has a tropical climate, making it a year-round tourist destination.

Tourist Attractions

Vivekananda Rock Memorial

A monument built in honor of Swami Vivekananda, located on a small island off the coast.

Thiruvalluvar Statue

A 133-foot statue of the Tamil poet and philosopher Thiruvalluvar, located on a rock off the shore.

Kanyakumari Beach

A famous beach where you can witness both the sunrise and sunset.

Tourism

Accommodation
